Little Story

Pfunda Tea Company Ltd is a limited liability company incorporated and domiciled in Rwanda. The principal shareholder Borelli Tea holding limited which is fully owned Subsidiary of Mc Leod Russell India limited incorporated in India. The company’s principal activities is the cultivation and processing black tea and other agricultural produce. Pfunda Tea Company , which is part of the Rwandan factories group, is a Rwandan company with roots in the country that date back over 48years to 1972. Pfunda Tea Company is medium scale tea processing company that process and export processed tea here in Rwanda and in foreign countries like USA and Germany.

This company has been founded by the Minister of Agriculture and Resources (MINAGRI) in its departement in charge of tea processing companies called OCIR Théa.

Pfunda Tea Company started by the name of Usine a Théa Pfunda , and as the time goes The government made privatization of PTC To the England's in 2004 that's when the name has been changed and again in 2014 also England's sold the Company to the Indians and up to now the company is hold by Indians.

The general objective of our system project is to help the administration of the company to analyze the record and information of their employees and maintaining easy communication between employees and admin of the system .
